Welcome to the new home of **The Daily Algorithm**.

​If you’ve been following our journey, you’ll notice things look different. We haven't just changed the paint; we’ve rebuilt the entire engine. This transition marks a shift from a standard news site to a **Technical Lab Journal** a space dedicated to documenting the raw intersection of AI, technical entrepreneurship, and market mechanics.

## ​Why We Built This

​The "traditional" way of blogging is slow, bloated, and often fragile. To truly cover the cutting edge of technology, we realized we needed to _use_ the cutting edge. By moving away from restrictive, legacy platforms and building a custom headless stack, we’ve gained three things: **Speed, Sovereignty, and Scale.**

### ​The Tech Stack

​For the developers and tech enthusiasts reading this, here is what is currently powering this platform:

- ​**Astro:** For lightning fast, content focused performance.
- ​**Tailwind CSS:** For a clean, professional, and adaptive UI that respects user system themes.
- ​**Supabase:** Handling our backend logic and real time interactions.
- ​**Decap CMS:** Allowing for a seamless, mobile first editorial workflow.

## ​What to Expect in the Lab

​This isn't a place for generic AI news. You can get that anywhere. **The Daily Algorithm** is now focused on high signal content across three main pillars:

1. ​**AI Experiments:** Real world documentation of AI tools, prompting frameworks (like Leonardo.ai), and automation workflows.
2. ​**Market Mechanics:** Deep dives into price action and the fundamentals of Forex trading, moving beyond the noise of "signals" into actual market literacy.
3. ​**Technical Entrepreneurship:** Transparent updates on projects like **Earnet** and the journey of transitioning from "vibe coding" to building production ready applications.

## ​A New Standard

​Technology moves at the speed of light. Your source for tech insights should do the same. This new platform is designed to be a living document of progress clean, professional, and data driven.

​The old site is gone. The lab is open. Let’s get to work.
